Corpus-level Document Similarity

The plot below visualizes the aggregate similarity of each passage in each document to other passages in the corpus. Similarity measures are calculated as follows: Each passage of each text is compared to all other passages, and the most similar passage is recorded. The mean of these "most-similar" passages is then plotted below.

The buttons below allow one to compare each document to "All" works in the corpus, only those published "Earlier", or only those published "Later" than itself. Taken collectively, these views offers a snapshot of the degree to which individual works derived from earlier works and/or influenced later works.
